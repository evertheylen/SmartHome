Home Page
How do i navigate?
You can navigate through our website using the manu icon in the top left of the screen. A drawer will open, and you'll be able to explore the magnificent world of OverWatch.
I heard there were extra features planned for the homepage, which features excactly?
In the future the home page might contain cool stuff like graphs that we think you might find important, statuses of friends you might find interesting, etc. You would also be able to mark graphs as important, and they would appear on your home page.
Those are just pour suggestions, it is up to the company that buys this website to decide what the homepage should do.
Statistics Page
The 4 dropdowns and their basic usage.
In the first dropdown box you can make a rough selection of the sensors you want to include in your graph.
There are three categories: Location, type, and tag. The sensors that have at least one elected location, one selected type and one selected tag will be included in the graph.

The second dropdown allows you to refine your selection made in the first box. You can view which sensors will be included in the graph and you can deselect them if you don't want them to be in the graph.

The third dropdown is how you want to view the graph. First you select the start and end date, which will define the length of the graph / the period of time you want to watch.
Then you'll have to choose how your data will be displayed:
Raw: The datapoints we kept in our database, the actual data generated by your sensors.
Days: View the energy consumption per day for the selection of sensors
Hours, years and months should be analogue to the days.
Eventually you can generate the graph pressing the "show data" button.

In the fourth dropdon you'll be able to view the graphs you generated in that session.
They disappear when you refresh the page. But you are able to share those graphs on your wall so you can look back at them later.
What does "aggregate by..." mean?
You can group sensors by selecting the "aggregate by..." checkboxes.
E.g. the aggregate by location checkbox groups all sensors that have the same location.

Adding a location/sensor
You add a location by pressing one of the "add..." buttons in the sensors page.
You just fill in the form with your information of the location/sensor.
Please note that the number of your location can't contain letters.
The description for your location is just for yourself, it's not that significant.
You don't have to give tags to your sensors, these are optional.
Editing a location/sensor
You can edit a location/sensor by pressing this button next to the sensor/location you want to edit: edit Editing is analogue to creating a new sensor/location. More about Adding a location/sensor
Deleting a location/sensor
You can delete a location/sensor by pressing this button next to the sensor/location you want to delete: delete PLEASE NOTE: all sensors connected to the location will be deleted. If a sensor gets removed, all its data will be gone.
Extra features
By pressing these arrows you can sort the rows alfabetically/numerical based on the columns: play_arrow

Making/joining a group.
To make a group, you'll have to press the matching button here: Give your group an awesome name and you're ready to rock!
You can join a group by pressing the button below the button to make a group. You'll then get a form where you can select the group you want to join.
Getting new friends.
You can find friends by pressing the matching button here: You'll get a list of people, and you'll be able to search for people using the search bar. You add them by clicking the "add friend" button.
Looking at the wall/profile of groups/friends
